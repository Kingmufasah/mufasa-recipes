Perfect Pasta Recipe

A Nice Plate of Pasta

This delicacy is a perfect blend of finesse and expertise, it's an Italian perfection prepared in the remote part of Africa in Jos, Nigeria. A blend of an array of ingredients to generate a mouth-watering experience, follow me while we succintly describe the process of creation.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 Satchet of spaghetti
  • 6 pieces of tomatoes
  • 2 pieces of onions
  • 5 pieces of pepper
  • 3 pieces of spring onions
  • 1 satchet of tomato paste
  • 1 piece of smoked fish
  • 6 pieces of maggi cubes
  • Chopped carrot and green beans
  • Groundnut oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Water

STEPS

  1. Put a bowl of water in the pot and let it heat up for 2mins
  2. Break the spaghetti into the pot and cover for 7mins
  3. Sieve content of pot and cover
  4. While that happens, cut the onions and spring onions blend the tomatoes and pepper
  5. Prepare the fish, take out all the bones
  6. Wash the chopped carrot and green beans and boil
  7. Boil the blended tomatoes and pepper until water is dried up
  8. Put a pot on the cooker at a low temperature, add groundnut oil and let fry for 15secs
  9. Add chopped onions to fry and immediately add blended tomatoes and peper
  10. Add tomato paste to fry
  11. Add fish to fry
  12. Add cubes of maggi
  13. Add a small quantity of water
  14. Add a small quantity of salt and let it simmer for 1min
  15. Add spaghetti, turn and let cook for 6-10mins
  16. Spread chopped carrot and green beans and spring onions on it on spaghetti at 3mins
